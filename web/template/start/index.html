The Amazing Trace

How to play:

  • You have one hour to scan as many Amazing Trace posters as you can.
  • You will be given clues to help you find the next posters.
  • You must unlock the clue for a poster before you can scan it.
  • Scan the poster or follow the URL to get your next clue.
  • You may ask for help from people around campus.
  • Whichever team scans the most posters wins.
